Wicked Dogs Hot Dog Joint serves the best hotdogs in Manila.

With humble beginnings in BF Homes, Wicked Dogs continues to satisfy everyone’s hotdog cravings and surprise adventurous foodies with crazy and over-the-top combinations inspired by sandwiches from all over the world.

Opened in 2015 and now two-branches strong, Wicked Dogs has consistently been a favorite of kids and adults alike with a simple and fun menu that’s sure to knock that hunger out of the park.
